Hometown

Surabaya

Surabaya is a city located in East Java province, Indonesia. It is the second-largest city in the country, after Jakarta, and has a population of approximately 3 million people. Surabaya is a significant economic, cultural, and educational center in East Java and is often referred to as the "City of Heroes" due to its role in Indonesia's struggle for independence.
